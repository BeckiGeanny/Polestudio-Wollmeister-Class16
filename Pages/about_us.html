About Us

Who are we? We are Polerina Pole Dance, your favorite Pole studio! ;) We are 4 passionate pole dancers with different specialties but with one thing in common: we LOVE to teach you how to dance, be sensual, be strong, flexible and confident. Years of training and competing have made us to who we are and we are proud of every bruise, because if there is one thing we pole dancers have in common, no matter if beginner or pro? We honour our bruises, they show the dedication that we put in to get the pose, choreo or routine right and we are proud of that! Each of us has a different way of teaching and together we have a perfect combination of technique, dance and fun.
But let’s introduce you to us personally: We are Miranda, Lena, Ryan and Alexandra.

Miranda

Miranda started dancing when she was just able to walk and didn’t stop since then. With 18 she found a new love of her life, the pole, and competed in various competitions around the world.

Lena

Lena came across the pole in her teenage years and trained hard to become a pro dancer and full blood instructor. She is our best motivator and you might have seen her in different shows around the country.

Ryan

Ryan keeps the mood up and has the strength to keep us all on his shoulders. His unique style includes strong movements and you will definitely train muscles that you didn’t know exited before.

Alexandra

And finally Alexandra who brings some hotness to the group. No movement seems edgy or silly, no matter if it’s just a normal walk or a spin, it is never just normal.
