Pole Classes

Exotic Pole

Exotic Pole is the most sensual form of pole dance. The focus lays on the dancing with and around the pole, even though pole poses are included in this form, a big part is also done on the floor. Usually, it is danced with high platform high heels.

Pole Fitness

Pole fitness is an extension of pole dancing that uses the pole as a fitness equipment. It is a whole-body workout using your body weight. Pole fitness is a pure workout session that might include some poses but choreographies or routines are not part of it.

Pole Dance

Pole dance is the most complete form of dancing around the pole. It combines dance moves with artistic poses on the pole. It requires strength and flexibility and good coordination as well as sensuality.

Stretching

To get good flexibility and prevent injuries, stretching is a very important part in the pole dance world. Having good flexibility will increase the possibilities on the pole as well as in all forms of dancing.
