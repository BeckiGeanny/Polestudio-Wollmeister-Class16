What is Pole dance?

Pole dance has a variety of styles and facettes. It can be sensual, artistic, creative and a lot of fun! Pole dance can be seen as a sport, a dance or something a little naughty. Pole dance is a mix of strength, flexibility, coordination and self-confidence. Get to know your body in a whole new way. This is not just about getting another work out in, it is about learning to love every part of your body and seeing what astonishing things it can do. No matter why you choose to do pole dancing, the most important thing is that you enjoy what you do.
